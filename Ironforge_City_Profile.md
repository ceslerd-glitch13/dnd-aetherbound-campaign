# Ironforge City Profile

**Status**: Primary Dwarven Gate City  
**Population**: ~42,000 (mostly dwarven with human and gnome minorities)  
**Racial Breakdown**: Dwarf ~75%, Human ~15%, Gnome ~7%, Other ~3%  
**Primary Economy**: Mining, heavy industry, weapons & armor, and dimensional gate trade  
**Government**: Clan Council led by a High Thane

---

## Leadership

**Primary Ruler**  
- **High Thane Thrain Ironfist** (Dwarf male, Lawful Neutral)  
  Stern but fair leader. Strongly focused on security and industrial output.

**Key Council / Figureheads**
- **Master Engineer Garrick Stonehammer** (Dwarf male, Neutral) – Oversees all mining operations and gate engineering.
- **Forge Mistress Helga Emberforge** (Dwarf female, Lawful Good) – Leads the great forges and weapons production.
- **Captain of the Deep Guard Drogath Stonefist** (Dwarf male, Lawful Neutral) – Commands the elite gate defenders.
- **Trade Envoy Vorn Ironvein** (Dwarf male, Neutral) – Handles external trade and relations with Ravensport.
- **Lorekeeper Brannok Runebeard** (Dwarf male, Neutral Good) – Keeper of ancient records and advisor on threats.

---

## Key Locations & Named Proprietors

**Inns & Taverns**
- **The Anvil’s Rest** – Loud, hearty inn popular with miners and soldiers. Run by **Innkeeper Helga Emberforge**.
- **The Deep Hearth** – Quieter tavern favored by engineers and scholars. Run by **Barkeep Brannok Runebeard**.

**Shops**
- **The Great Forge** – Weapons, armor, and siege equipment. Proprietor: **Forge Mistress Helga Emberforge**.
- **Stonehammer Engineering** – Mining equipment and mechanical devices. Proprietor: **Master Engineer Garrick Stonehammer**.
- **Rune & Relic** – Magical items and ancient dwarven artifacts. Proprietor: **Lorekeeper Brannok Runebeard**.
- **Ironvein Traders** – General goods and dimensional imports. Proprietor: **Trader Vorn Ironvein**.
- **Deepward Armory** – Elite armor and weapons for the Deep Guard. Proprietor: **Captain Drogath Stonefist**.
- **Gemcutter’s Hall** – Fine gems and jewelry. Proprietor: **Gemcutter Lirael Gemheart** (Dwarf female).

---

## District Breakdown

**The Gate Heart**  
Massive fortified underground complex containing the dimensional gate. Most military power is here.

**The Forges**  
Industrial heart of the city. Constant sound of hammers and machinery.

**The Deep Mines**  
Extensive mining tunnels beneath and around the city.

**The Clan Halls**  
Residential and political districts for the various dwarven clans.

**The Trade Caverns**  
Market district for both local and dimensional goods.

---

## Economy
- Heavy focus on **mining, metalworking, and engineering**.
- Dimensional gate trade brings in exotic ores and technology.
- Strong industrial output (weapons, tools, machinery).

---

## Defenses
- Extremely thick stone walls and multiple layers of underground fortifications.
- Elite Deep Guard (highly trained and heavily armored).
- Extensive use of mechanical traps and siege engines.
- Strong alliance with Ravensport for gate security.
- Regular patrols through the mines and surrounding mountains.

---

## Immediate Plot Hooks
1. A major tunnel collapse has trapped miners near the gate complex.
2. Strange mechanical failures have been occurring around the gate.
3. Tensions between traditionalist clans and those pushing for more trade with humans.
4. Reports of unknown creatures coming through the gate at night.
5. The High Thane is looking for capable adventurers to investigate sabotage in the deep mines.
