## The Hook – Captain Elandor “Blackscale” Rurik

**Where the players meet him**: The Rusty Anchor tavern & inn (Docks district). It’s a lively, slightly rowdy place popular with sailors, adventurers, and merchants.

**Full Description of The Rusty Anchor** (for DM reference):  
The Rusty Anchor is a large, two-story wooden building right on the riverfront. A large, slightly crooked anchor hangs above the main door. Inside, the common room is always busy. The air smells of salt, ale, roasted meat, and pipe smoke. It’s loud, warm, and lively.

**Staff**:
- **Captain Elandor “Blackscale” Rurik** (Owner) – Grizzled former sailor with a black eyepatch.
- **Mira “Red” Tanner** (Head Bartender & Cook) – Tough, no-nonsense woman with bright red hair.
- **Jorik Stonehand** (Bouncer & Handyman) – Broad dwarf who breaks up fights.
- **Lirael "Silverthorn"** (Waitress & Information Broker, Half-elf) – Friendly and chatty. She is a distant relative of the ruling Voss family but hides this connection. She uses the name "Silverthorn" publicly and gathers intelligence for her family while enjoying the dockside chaos. One of the best sources of rumors in the Docks.
- Young staff (busboys, etc.).

**Menu**: Rusty Anchor Ale, grilled river fish, salted pork stew, sailor’s platter, spiced rum drinks.

---

**Rurik’s Pitch** (read or paraphrase):

> “Three days ago I was hired to bring a small, unassuming shipment from one of the outer ports straight to Ravensport. Nothing flashy — just an ordinary-looking silver necklace in a locked box. The client paid half upfront and promised the rest on delivery.  
> 
> Last night, right after we docked, the box was stolen from my strongroom. No signs of forced entry. I’m certain it was an inside job or someone who knew exactly what they were after.  
> 
> The problem is… the final client is **very** important and very impatient. If I don’t recover that necklace, I’m not getting the rest of my payment — and I might lose my ship.  
> 
> Find who took it, get the necklace back, and there’s a fat reward in it for you. I don’t care how you do it. Just bring me that damn necklace.”

**Reward**:
- 200 gp total (50 gp upfront, 150 gp on return)
- Free room & board at The Rusty Anchor for a month
- Future favors from Captain Rurik

---

## The Resonance Medallion (The Stolen Item)

- Looks like an ordinary silver necklace.
- Contains a hidden smooth blue crystal (no runes).
- Once worn by a spellcaster, it grants the buffer effect (keeps prepared spells inside dead zones, shortens durations, etc.).
- No attunement required — simply wearing it works.
