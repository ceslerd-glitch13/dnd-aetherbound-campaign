# Source and Edition Guidance

> **Repository role:** Supporting guidance for [catalog index](../Creature_Catalog_Index.md).

## Authority boundary

The catalog records a statistics baseline so the correct owned official entry can be located. It does not reproduce a complete published stat block and does not replace the campaign's custom combat authority at `../../combat/Enemy_Encounters_Stat_Blocks.md`.

The campaign remains based on D&D 5th Edition with targeted homebrew. A creature entry may reference a 2014 source, a 2024 source, or a later official supplement without changing the campaign-wide rules foundation.

## Selection order

When preparing an encounter:

1. Confirm that the creature fits the intended region, gate incident, ecology, or story.
2. Select the exact owned official version before play.
3. Use that version consistently for the encounter.
4. Apply only dead-zone rules approved in `../../canon/DnD_Campaign_World_Bible.md`.
5. Create or update a campaign-specific stat block only when the creature is materially altered, recurring, or cannot be run from the selected official entry.

Lore fit, region, encounter purpose, and Dungeon Master approval outrank edition preference. Importing one creature version does not silently alter the rest of the campaign's rules.
