# Roll Table Selection Guide

Use this guide only to choose a table. After rolling, follow the result links into the creature catalog.

## Dead Zones

Prefer a physically self-sustaining natural or mechanical table unless the encounter has a clear reason to involve a magically dependent creature.

After the roll, the linked catalog entry and `../guidance/Dead_Zone_Creature_Guidance.md` determine what remains functional. The roll table does not create a dead-zone exception.

## Gate Incursions

Select by the scale and nature of the breach:

- [Minor Elemental Gate Invasions](gate_incursions/Minor_Elemental_Gate_Invasions.md)
- [Major Elemental Gate Invasions](gate_incursions/Major_Elemental_Gate_Invasions.md)
- [Minor and Moderate Gate Invasions](gate_incursions/Minor_and_Moderate_Gate_Invasions.md)
- [Severe Gate Invasions](gate_incursions/Severe_Gate_Invasions.md)

Every result is a large-scale force of at least 50 creatures. Treat it as a scenario or objective generator, not a routine random combat.
