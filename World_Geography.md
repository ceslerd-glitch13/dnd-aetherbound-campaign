# World Geography

**Continent Name**: (To be named — suggestions: Eldoria, Aetheria, etc.)

**Overall Shape**: A large, somewhat irregular continent with varied terrain — mountains in the north and west, central forests and hills, southern plains and coasts.

---

## Major Regions

### Far North
- Heavy snow, snowy plains, two main mountain ranges with a hilly curve around the plains.
- Cold and harsh environment.
- Small elven outposts exist, but most elves avoid this area due to the cold.
- Some dwarven and human frontier settlements.

### Central Band (East to West)
- Temperate forests, rivers, and rolling hills.
- **Primary home of most elves**. They thrive here due to harmony with nature.
- Mixed population with human and dwarven trading outposts.

### South
- Warmer plains, rivers, and coastal areas.
- **Primary home of most humans**. They prefer open land and trade, keeping largely to themselves but acting as central trading partners for the continent.

### West of Ravensport (Human Gate City)
- Significant mountain range.
- Contains a **large dwarven mining settlement** (not a full city, but a major mining town). Dwarves dominate underground here due to darkvision.

---

## Dead Zones (10 Total)
- Scattered across the continent.
- **One dead zone contains Deepanvil** (a major dwarven mining/trade city in the central-west mountains). Dwarves use it as a protective shield against enemy spellcasters and are not particularly concerned about the lack of magic. It is a known trading city due to a rare mineral they mine. Spellcasters who visit feel uneasy and ill.
- **2–3 dead zones** are in the middle of nowhere (no effect on trade routes).
- The remaining dead zones are scattered. Most major trade routes **go around** dead zones (there is almost always a faster route by avoiding them), but some city paths have dead zones along them.
- One dead zone is approximately **30 minutes by fast horse** (~2 hours walking) from Ravensport.

---

**Hidden Plot Note (Deepanvil)**: The dwarves are unknowingly digging toward (or near) one of the Aetheron nodes/beacons while mining.
