# Ravensport City Profile

**Status**: Major Trade Hub & Dimensional Gate City  
**Population**: ~48,000 (permanent) + heavy transient trader population  
**Racial Breakdown**: Human ~55%, Elf ~20%, Dwarf ~15%, Other ~10%  
**Primary Economy**: Dimensional trade through the gate + regional trade hub  
**Government**: Lord Protector with City Council

---

## Leadership

**Primary Ruler**  
- **Lord Protector Harlan Voss** (Human male, Lawful Neutral)  
  Aging but respected ruler. Focuses on keeping the gate secure and trade flowing.

**Key Council / Figureheads**
- **High Stewardess Elara Voss** (Human female, Lawful Good) – Harlan’s daughter. Handles day-to-day administration.
- **Gate Warden Commander Seraphine Vale** (Elf female, Lawful Neutral) – Oversees all gate security and the Iron Talons.
- **Master Merchant Guild Leader Vorn Ironvein** (Dwarf male, Neutral) – Represents merchant interests.
- **Archmage Lirael Starveil** (Elf female, Neutral Good) – Leads the Arcane Wardens and advises on magical threats.
- **Captain of the City Guard Thorne Blackwood** (Human male, Lawful Neutral) – Commands regular city patrols.

---

## Key Locations & Named Proprietors

**Inns & Taverns**
- **The Rusty Anchor** – Popular with sailors, caravan guards, and adventurers. Run by **Captain Rurik Voss** (retired ship captain, Human male).
- **The Gilded Gate** – Upscale inn favored by wealthy traders and nobles. Run by **Innkeeper Selene Brightwater** (Half-elf female).

**Shops**
- **Arcane Emporium** – Magical components, scrolls, and minor enchanted items. Proprietor: **Archmage Lirael Starveil** (also serves as advisor).
- **Iron & Thread** – Armor, clothing, and tailoring. Proprietor: **Master Armorer Thrain Stonehand** (Dwarf male).
- **Blade & Bolt** – Weapons and crossbows. Proprietor: **Weaponsmith Garrick Ironclad** (Dwarf male).
- **Essence & Elixir** – Alchemical supplies and potions. Proprietor: **Apothecary Mirael Thornwood** (Human female).
- **Last Chance Curios** – Pawn shop and fence for questionable goods. Proprietor: **Fence Vesper Crowe** (Human male).
- **The Caravan Outfitter** – Travel gear, wagons, and supplies. Proprietor: **Trader Harlan Reed** (Human male).

---

## District Breakdown

**Docks District**  
River port with 4 piers. Heavy trade and sailor activity. The Rusty Anchor is located here.

**Grand Bazaar**  
Main trade quarter with permanent shops and daily rotating stalls. Heart of the city’s economy.

**Gate Quarter (The Heart)**  
Heavily fortified inner complex containing the dimensional gate. Most military power is concentrated here.

**Shrouded Wards**  
Lower district with higher crime. Home to the black market and many criminal elements.

**Residential District**  
Where most common citizens live. Mix of housing from modest to wealthy.

**Noble Hill**  
Smaller, well-defended area for nobles and wealthy merchants.

---

## Economy
- Primary wealth comes from **dimensional gate trade**.
- Acts as a middleman between dimensions and the rest of the continent.
- Secondary economies: Fishing, farming (outside city), and general trade.

---

## Defenses
- Massive fortified walls around the Gate Quarter.
- 8 watchtowers along the inner walls.
- ~1,800 total military personnel (1,000+ concentrated at the gate).
- Elite Iron Talons (200) + Arcane Wardens (80 spellcasters).
- Strong anti-magic and anti-teleportation wards around the gate.
- Regular patrols throughout the city.

---

## Immediate Plot Hooks
1. Captain Rurik has lost an important shipment (starter quest).
2. Strange activity reported near one of the nearby dead zones.
3. A merchant claims someone is smuggling dangerous items through the gate.
4. Tensions rising between city guards and dock workers over wages.
5. Rumors of a powerful magical artifact being brought through the gate.
