# NPC Backstory & Personality File — Master Database

## Purpose

This is the master identity and roleplaying reference for named NPCs. City profiles provide immediate local context; this file owns each NPC's canonical name, role, personality, and recurring relationships.

## Usage Rules

- Use a unique full name for every unrelated recurring NPC.
- Repeated surnames should indicate an intentional family, clan, or organizational connection.
- Major leaders do not personally operate ordinary retail counters unless the connection is intentional and clearly stated.
- Important leaders and proprietors receive a three-line profile:
  - **Appearance**
  - **Personality + Role**
  - **Story Hook**
- Minor employees and one-scene NPCs may remain unnamed until play makes them important.
- Titles describe authority, not character level. Combat statistics belong in `Enemy_Encounters_Stat_Blocks.md`.

# Ravensport

## Government and Gate Leadership

### Lord Protector Elara Voss

- **Appearance:** Human woman in a tailored navy coat with a silver civic chain, usually carrying a slim folder of reports rather than a ceremonial weapon.
- **Personality + Role:** Calm, compassionate, and administratively relentless; she chairs the City Council and governs Ravensport's civil affairs.
- **Story Hook:** Can authorize permits, investigations, and emergency resources, but currently needs reliable evidence of corruption affecting Heart access and cargo priority.

### Harlan Voss — Retired Lord Protector

- **Appearance:** Elderly human man with a carved walking cane, an old gate-defense scar across one hand, and the habit of dressing more plainly than his former station.
- **Personality + Role:** Blunt, strategic, and protective of Elara; he advises privately but no longer holds a vote or operational command.
- **Story Hook:** Remembers old gate incidents, political compromises, and families whose influence predates the current council.

### Gate Warden Seraphine Vale

- **Appearance:** Tall elven woman with silver-braided hair and an immaculate gatewarden uniform marked with restrained protective runes.
- **Personality + Role:** Precise, disciplined, and difficult to impress; she controls gate-security policy and strategic oversight of the Iron Talons.
- **Story Hook:** Can approve restricted Heart access and is quietly concerned that recent incursions show signs of deliberate testing rather than animal aggression.

### Master of Trade Vorn Ironvein

- **Appearance:** Broad dwarven man in expensive but practical merchant clothing, with a gem-counting frame clipped to his belt.
- **Personality + Role:** Pragmatic, patient, and skilled at finding the cost hidden inside every proposal; he represents guilds, tariffs, logistics, and major commerce.
- **Story Hook:** Knows which cargo backlogs are genuine and which were manufactured to benefit smugglers, officials, or favored guilds.

### Archmage Lirael Starveil

- **Appearance:** Elven woman in layered blue-gray robes with ink-stained gloves and a cluster of small diagnostic crystals at her waist.
- **Personality + Role:** Curious, careful, and quietly intense; she leads the Arcane Wardens and advises on planar and magical threats.
- **Story Hook:** Can identify unusual magic or Aetherite technology, but may require samples, controlled testing, or a dangerous visit to the Heart.

### Captain Thorne Blackwood

- **Appearance:** Weathered human man in a black patrol coat with a well-used sword and a notebook full of names, addresses, and unfinished investigations.
- **Personality + Role:** Direct, fair, and skeptical of political excuses; he commands ordinary city law enforcement outside the gate fortress.
- **Story Hook:** Can provide warrants, patrol support, or criminal intelligence if the party helps him separate ordinary smuggling from a civilization-level threat.

### Gate Commander Kael Thorne

- **Appearance:** Scarred human officer in reinforced plate designed to remain useful under anti-magic conditions.
- **Personality + Role:** Terse, tactical, and focused on containment; he commands the Gate Heart and Iron Talons during operations.
- **Story Hook:** Controls practical access to restricted defenses and may recruit proven adventurers when an incursion behaves in a way his soldiers have never seen.

## Major Proprietors and Service Contacts

### Captain Elandor "Blackscale" Rurik — The Rusty Anchor

- **Appearance:** Grizzled human with a black eyepatch, weathered sea coat, anchor tattoo, and the posture of someone who still expects a deck to move beneath him.
- **Personality + Role:** Blunt but fair semi-retired ship captain and tavern proprietor; protective of his crew, ship, livelihood, and reputation.
- **Story Hook:** The unmarked Aetherite orb hidden in the Resonance Medallion was stolen from his strongroom before delivery to Silas Crowe.

### Mira “Red” Tanner — Rusty Anchor Head Bartender and Cook

- **Appearance:** Tough human woman with bright red hair, rolled sleeves, and a kitchen knife she handles with professional ease.
- **Personality + Role:** No-nonsense, protective, and observant; she runs the Rusty Anchor's bar and kitchen when Rurik is occupied.
- **Story Hook:** Remembers who asked about the ship's unloading schedule and can tell when a supposed sailor does not know dockside work.

### Jorik Broadshoulder — Rusty Anchor Bouncer and Handyman

- **Appearance:** Broad dwarven man with a repaired nose, heavy tool belt, and arms usually crossed near the tavern door.
- **Personality + Role:** Quiet, patient, and difficult to provoke; he handles repairs, security, and drunken disputes.
- **Story Hook:** Saw a covered handcart leave toward the Shrouded Wards after the theft and can identify the route it took.

### Selene Brightwater — The Gilded Gate

- **Appearance:** Polished half-elven woman in understated formal clothing who remembers faces, titles, and preferred drinks after one meeting.
- **Personality + Role:** Gracious, socially perceptive, and discreet; she operates Ravensport's premier inn for wealthy merchants and officials.
- **Story Hook:** Hears private negotiations and can arrange introductions, but never shares information without gaining protection, leverage, or a future favor.

### Avenna Quillstar — Arcane Emporium

- **Appearance:** Middle-aged human mage with brass spectacles, neatly bound sleeves, and licensed inventory seals hanging from every locked cabinet.
- **Personality + Role:** Exacting, knowledgeable, and cautious; she sells legal components, scrolls, identification, and minor enchantment services.
- **Story Hook:** Can identify restricted magical goods, but is legally required to report undeclared Aetherite devices and suspicious planar artifacts.

### Dorrin Stonehand — Iron & Thread

- **Appearance:** Compact dwarven armorer in a divided leather-and-cloth apron, constantly checking seams, rivets, and balance.
- **Personality + Role:** Cheerful perfectionist who repairs armor, clothing, uniforms, and protective travel gear.
- **Story Hook:** Recently noticed forged guard coats using the correct fabric but outdated internal stitching known only to an older supplier.

### Torren Ironclad — Blade & Bolt

- **Appearance:** Muscular dwarven weaponsmith with a shaved head, heat-darkened forearms, and several testing scars across his shield hand.
- **Personality + Role:** Competitive, proud, and honest about quality; he sells and repairs weapons, crossbows, and licensed defensive equipment.
- **Story Hook:** Can identify the workshop that produced a weapon from its balance and tool marks, making him useful in murder or smuggling investigations.

### Mirael Thornwood — Essence & Elixir

- **Appearance:** Human apothecary with braided brown hair, herb-stained fingers, and a satchel organized more carefully than most military kits.
- **Personality + Role:** Practical, kind, and intolerant of careless dosing; she provides medicine, antidotes, alchemical goods, and mundane treatment.
- **Story Hook:** Several dockworkers recently developed the same unfamiliar symptoms after unloading a supposedly ordinary dimensional shipment.

### Corvin Rook — Last Chance Curios

- **Appearance:** Thin human man in layered secondhand finery with restless eyes and too many keys.
- **Personality + Role:** Charming, evasive, and professionally cautious; he runs a pawnshop and low-level fencing operation.
- **Story Hook:** Knows who is moving stolen cargo through the Shrouded Wards, but fears Nyra Vell more than he fears the city guard.

### Mara Reed — The Caravan Outfitter

- **Appearance:** Broad-shouldered human woman with a weatherproof coat, route maps tucked into her belt, and chalk marks covering one sleeve.
- **Personality + Role:** Brisk, organized, and deeply practical; she supplies caravans and arranges wagons, animals, guides, and escorts.
- **Story Hook:** Can hire the party for western routes and knows which recent caravan losses look like banditry, mechanical failure, or deliberate concealment.

## Early-Campaign and Criminal NPCs

### Maelis "Silverthorn" — Rusty Anchor Information Broker

- **Appearance:** Friendly half-elven server with silver-threaded hair, a quick tray hand, and eyes that continually measure the room.
- **Personality + Role:** Chatty, observant, and independent; she gathers information for Elara Voss while working openly at the Rusty Anchor.
- **Story Hook:** Can connect the party to rumors about the medallion, Shrouded Wards smugglers, western caravans, and unusual blue crystals.

**Extended details:** Maelis wants to prove that informal intelligence can protect the city better than rigid bureaucracy. She is a distant Voss relative but does not advertise the connection. Through private family contact, she once saw an unmarked Aetherite power orb and heard enough restricted rumors to recognize the category of object, though she does not know its exact charge rules or Veyne's transit plan. When excited, she mixes nautical slang with old elven expressions.

### Silas Crowe — Veyne's Agent

- **Appearance:** Immaculately dressed human fixer with a silver ring containing a blue crystal inlay and one or two discreet bodyguards nearby.
- **Personality + Role:** Calculating, formal, and impatient; he arranges acquisitions and logistics for Lord Cassian Veyne's organization.
- **Story Hook:** Commissioned Rurik's delivery and will pursue the orb through negotiation, pressure, or violence if the party keeps it.

**Extended details:** Silas never uses contractions. He understands that the unmarked orb is a battery for Veyne's three-crystal travel array and conceals that purpose behind commercial language.

### Nyra "the Thread" Vell — Shrouded Wards Smuggling Leader

- **Appearance:** Unassuming half-elven woman in layered scarves and practical clothes, with a thin cheek scar and a mechanical trinket always moving through her fingers.
- **Personality + Role:** Charismatic, patient, and ruthless; she controls a network moving restricted goods, documents, and information through the city.
- **Story Hook:** May have arranged or benefited from the medallion theft and can become an enemy, reluctant source, or temporary ally.

**Extended details:** Nyra values control of routes more than individual cargo. She has dealt with Veyne's agents but does not trust them and suspects their plans threaten the criminal economy she depends upon.

### Orren Pike — Frightened Dockworker and Immediate Thief

- **Appearance:** Thin human dockworker with cracked hands, borrowed work clothes, and the exhausted look of someone who has not slept since making a bad decision.
- **Personality + Role:** Nervous, ashamed, and motivated by debt rather than ideology; he copied Rurik's key and stole one specific box for a smuggling intermediary.
- **Story Hook:** Believed the cargo was jewelry or contraband and can identify the runner, false badge, payment method, and Shrouded Wards transfer point.

### Brogan Ashdelve — Deepanvil Caravan Liaison

- **Appearance:** Stocky dwarven man with a soot-streaked beard, reinforced work gloves, and a mining pick used as both tool and walking staff.
- **Personality + Role:** Gruff, practical, and protective of his crews; he coordinates supply runs between Ravensport and Deepanvil.
- **Story Hook:** Can direct the party toward western caravan work, mine emergencies, and suspicious mechanical failures below Deepanvil.

**Extended details:** Brogan tests people through practical tasks rather than conversation. He suspects someone is concealing the cause of recent failures but does not yet understand their connection to deeper Aetherite activity.

# Faerindel

## Leadership

### High Warden Sylvara Leafwhisper

- **Appearance:** Ancient elven woman in layered green robes, carrying a living-wood staff that flowers differently with the season.
- **Personality + Role:** Patient, perceptive, and firm when harmony is threatened; she leads Faerindel's Council of Elders.
- **Story Hook:** Can open political or sacred doors, but wants proof that outsiders understand the consequences of whatever assistance they request.

### Archdruidess Elowen Silverthread

- **Appearance:** Silver-haired elf with soil on her hands, living vines braided into her sleeves, and a calm expression that rarely changes.
- **Personality + Role:** Compassionate, cautious, and fiercely protective of natural systems; she oversees druidic orders, agriculture, and ecological health.
- **Story Hook:** Needs discreet help investigating corruption near a dead-zone boundary without causing panic or damaging the affected land.

### Master Enchanter Thalorien Starweaver

- **Appearance:** Slender elven man in geometric robes, surrounded by hovering measuring instruments that vanish inside anti-magic areas.
- **Personality + Role:** Analytical, reserved, and exacting; he leads the Arcane Academy and magical gate-security research.
- **Story Hook:** Can grant access to advanced enchantment or planar research if the party recovers data from a location where active magic cannot be trusted.

### Captain Caelyra Moonveil

- **Appearance:** Athletic elven woman in flexible silver-green armor with a longbow and a plain anti-magic backup blade.
- **Personality + Role:** Alert, decisive, and respectful of competence; she commands Faerindel's Wardens and gate defenders.
- **Story Hook:** Seeks outsiders when a threat requires methods her magically trained Wardens may not possess.

### Trade Envoy Saelith Brightsong

- **Appearance:** Half-elven man in bright but practical diplomatic clothing, carrying trade tokens from all three major peoples.
- **Personality + Role:** Charming, adaptable, and politically careful; he manages foreign trade and relations with Ravensport and Ironforge.
- **Story Hook:** Can arrange licenses and introductions, but currently needs neutral agents to investigate whether a trade dispute is incompetence, corruption, or deliberate manipulation.

### Lorekeeper Arannis Dawnwhisper

- **Appearance:** Elder elven scholar with amber spectacles, a robe lined with written memory strips, and a habit of pausing before answering.
- **Personality + Role:** Thoughtful, precise, and protective of dangerous knowledge; he maintains the city's historical and planar archives.
- **Story Hook:** Recognizes patterns in old gate incidents and may reveal restricted lore if the party demonstrates that withholding it would be more dangerous.

- **Personality + Role:** Relaxed, witty, and observant; she runs a tavern favored by scholars, rangers, and off-duty Wardens.
- **Story Hook:** Hears arguments between traditionalists and reformers before they become public and can identify who is quietly recruiting support.

### Aelith Silverleaf — The Living Loom

- **Appearance:** Graceful elven weaver in color-shifting clothing with silver needles arranged through her hair.
- **Personality + Role:** Artistic, exacting, and easily offended by waste; she creates fine mundane and enchanted fabrics.
- **Story Hook:** Recently received a commission using fibers harvested from a protected source and wants the supplier identified without damaging her reputation.

### Rhaelor Emberbranch — Starforge Atelier

- **Appearance:** Lean elven smith with heat-resistant sleeves, a burn scar along one cheek, and protective lenses resting above his eyes.
- **Personality + Role:** Inventive, proud, and practical; he operates the public atelier under licenses established by Thalorien Starweaver.
- **Story Hook:** Can craft or repair magical weapons, but needs help recovering a stolen design that could bypass part of the Heart's ward network.

### Maera Thornwhisper — Verdant Apothecary

- **Appearance:** Soft-spoken elven woman with green-stained fingertips, bundles of dried herbs, and a small brass surgical kit.
- **Personality + Role:** Gentle, methodical, and scientifically minded; she blends mundane medicine, alchemy, and nature magic.

### Ilyan Quillshade — The Eternal Quill

- **Appearance:** Thin elven bookseller in layered gray robes, with ink on his chin and catalog cards tucked into every pocket.
- **Personality + Role:** Curious, absent-minded, and extremely protective of rare texts; he manages public sales and research requests connected to the city archives.
- **Story Hook:** Knows who recently sought forbidden material about gate failures but cannot disclose the record without lawful cause or leverage.

### Perrin Brightpath — Gateward Traders

- **Appearance:** Neatly dressed half-elven merchant with a portable scale, multilingual labels, and a bright copper badge of license.
- **Personality + Role:** Friendly, fast-talking, and scrupulous about paperwork; he sells approved dimensional goods and imported machinery.
- **Story Hook:** A legal shipment was replaced with convincing counterfeits after passing inspection, and he needs investigators who are not tied to the customs office.

### Elira Moonwhisper — Moonstone Jewels

- **Appearance:** Elder elven jeweler with luminous lenses, steady hands, and a necklace displaying stones from several planes.
- **Personality + Role:** Patient, private, and fascinated by protective design; she crafts jewelry, amulets, and precision settings.
- **Story Hook:** Can recognize Aetherite workmanship from its internal structure and may notice that an apparently decorative crystal is actually a functional component.

# Ironforge

## Leadership

### High Thane Thrain Ironfist

- **Appearance:** Massive dwarven man with an iron-gray beard, ceremonial armor, and a plain work hammer carried beside his badge of office.
- **Personality + Role:** Stern, fair, and relentlessly practical; he leads the Clan Council and balances gate defense with industrial needs.
- **Story Hook:** Can mobilize major resources but expects proof, accountability, and a plan that does not waste dwarven lives or materials.

### Master Engineer Garrick Stonehammer

- **Appearance:** Thick-armed dwarf with brass drafting tools, soot-darkened goggles, and mechanical chalk marks covering his coat.
- **Personality + Role:** Brilliant, impatient, and happiest inside a functioning machine; he oversees gate engineering, mines, and major infrastructure.
- **Story Hook:** Needs outside help testing whether recent system failures are design flaws, altered components, or evidence that someone understands restricted plans.

### Forge Mistress Helga Emberforge

- **Appearance:** Powerful dwarven woman with copper rings braided into her beard, heat-scarred gloves, and a hammer marked with layered runes.
- **Personality + Role:** Demanding, honorable, and protective of her workers; she directs major forge production and integrated magical armament.
- **Story Hook:** Can commission or authorize exceptional equipment, but currently faces evidence that restricted enchantment methods escaped a secure workshop.

### Captain Drogath Stonefist

- **Appearance:** Square-built dwarf in heavy gate armor with a broken tusk-shaped ornament mounted on one shoulder.
- **Personality + Role:** Disciplined, suspicious, and fiercely loyal; he commands the Deep Guard and physical defense of the Gate Heart.
- **Story Hook:** May recruit adventurers for threats that cannot be addressed by a visible military deployment.

### Trade Envoy Varric Stonecoin

- **Appearance:** Immaculate dwarf in dark merchant leathers with clan contracts sealed in metal tubes.
- **Personality + Role:** Courteous, calculating, and skilled at turning obligations into agreements; he manages foreign trade and supply relations.
- **Story Hook:** Knows which shortages are real and which clans are using gate-security concerns to manipulate contracts.

### Lorekeeper Brannok Runebeard

- **Appearance:** Elder dwarf with runes braided into a long white beard and thick stone tablets carried in a reinforced case.
- **Personality + Role:** Dry-witted, stubborn, and deeply responsible; he preserves ancient records, designs, and warnings.
- **Story Hook:** Can identify lost dwarven or gate technology, but demands a convincing reason before releasing knowledge that once caused disaster.

- **Personality + Role:** Efficient, proud, and blunt about deadlines; she manages public commissions and masterwork orders under Helga Emberforge's authority.
- **Story Hook:** Can arrange exceptional crafting, but needs help locating a missing shipment of alloy officially recorded as delivered.

### Berrin Gearlock — Stonehammer Engineering

- **Appearance:** Narrow dwarven man with articulated finger braces, a tool harness, and a permanent smear of machine grease across his brow.
- **Personality + Role:** Inventive, excitable, and careless with sleep; he operates the public engineering workshop associated with Garrick Stonehammer's guild.

### Odrun Relicward — Rune & Relic

- **Appearance:** Heavyset dwarf in layered protective robes with one artificial eye designed to examine rune depth.
- **Personality + Role:** Cautious, scholarly, and respectful of dangerous objects; he studies and sells licensed relics and magical equipment.

### Tilda Coppermark — Ironvein Traders

- **Appearance:** Sharp-eyed dwarven woman with copper-marked ledgers and a small bell used to summon clerks.
- **Personality + Role:** Fast, disciplined, and unforgiving of bad accounting; she operates the historic trading house despite having no relation to Vorn Ironvein.
- **Story Hook:** Can locate imported goods and cargo records, but wants a discreet explanation for supplies disappearing between the gate ledger and warehouse floor.

### Hargin Shieldthane — Deepward Armory

- **Personality + Role:** Gruff, patriotic, and obsessive about reliability; he manages approved military and defensive equipment.

### Runa Gemheart — Gemcutter's Hall

- **Appearance:** Dwarven woman with magnifying lenses, jeweled ear cuffs, and hands steady enough to carve runes inside small stones.
- **Personality + Role:** Patient, ambitious, and fascinated by unfamiliar materials; she cuts gems and prepares precision crystal settings.
- **Story Hook:** Can examine Aetherite crystals without activating them and may identify evidence that several fragments originated from the same device.

# Deepanvil — Mining Settlement Inside a Dead Zone

## Leadership

### Chief Engineer Borin Stonefist

- **Appearance:** Older dwarf with a square beard, reinforced knee braces, and a work coat marked by decades of repaired tears.
- **Personality + Role:** Practical, decisive, and unwilling to gamble with worker safety; he leads Deepanvil's Mining Council.
- **Story Hook:** Can authorize mine access and emergency resources, but needs outsiders when clan politics prevent his own investigators from appearing neutral.

### Master Miner Korrin Deepdelve

- **Appearance:** Lean dwarf with pale mine scars, a lamp mounted to his helmet, and a habit of tapping stone while listening.
- **Personality + Role:** Patient, cautious, and respected by working miners; he oversees excavation and geological planning.
- **Story Hook:** Believes a newly opened depth contains worked structures rather than natural caverns and wants confirmation before production crews enter.

### Forge Overseer Helga Brassmantle

- **Appearance:** Broad dwarven woman in a brass-scaled work mantle, with soot-darkened hair and a voice that carries across a forge floor.
- **Personality + Role:** Fair, demanding, and fiercely protective of apprentices; she manages manufacturing and major forge operations.
- **Story Hook:** Can supply excellent equipment but is investigating why several supposedly perfected alloy tools failed under ordinary mechanical stress.

### Security Captain Torag Stoneguard

- **Appearance:** Thick-necked dwarf in layered mechanical armor with signal whistles and tunnel keys across his chest.
- **Personality + Role:** Suspicious, methodical, and rescue-minded; he commands militia, mine security, and emergency response.
- **Story Hook:** Needs deniable help following someone who knows how to bypass physical locks without using magic.

### Trade Factor Keldran Hammerfall

- **Appearance:** Well-fed dwarf in road-worn formal clothing, with caravan tokens and supply tallies attached to a chain.
- **Personality + Role:** Sociable, persistent, and skilled at maintaining relationships; he manages Deepanvil's surface trade.
- **Story Hook:** Can arrange transport and contracts, but urgently needs to know why food caravans are being delayed along routes that remain physically open.

### Head Engineer Odrik Gearwright

- **Appearance:** Elder dwarf with a magnifying monocle, articulated tool belt, and two fingers replaced by precision mechanical prosthetics.
- **Personality + Role:** Curious, patient, and quietly radical; he leads research into dead-zone engineering and high-efficiency mundane systems.
- **Story Hook:** Suspects recent malfunctions originate from a deeper power-harvesting structure rather than from Deepanvil machinery.
