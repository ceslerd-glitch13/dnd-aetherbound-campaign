# Early Campaign Plan: Sessions 0–5 (Focused Starter Arc)

**Campaign:** Untitled D&D Campaign  
**Target Player Level:** 1–4 (by end of Session 5)  
**Primary Location:** Ravensport and immediate surroundings (Docks, Grand Bazaar, Shrouded Wards, Gate Quarter). One optional path leads west toward Deepanvil.  
**Core Philosophy:** Sandbox-style play with a strong central hook (the Resonance Medallion theft). Multiple branching paths that players can pursue in any order or combination. All paths seed the larger Aetherbound / dead zone / Aetheron plot without railroading. Players may never leave Ravensport if they choose urban-focused play.  
**Design Notes**: 
- Follows all existing Guidelines (check Enemy_Encounters_Stat_Blocks.md first for any new threats; use standard stat block format; pre-create key NPCs per NPC Character Creation Guideline).
- Encounters use D&D Beyond / SRD / Monster Manual references for standard monsters (quick lookup during play). Partial homebrew adjustments noted for dead zones or campaign flavor. Some pre-filled skeletons for common Session 0–3 threats.
- Map: Textual descriptions and key location relationships prioritized. Visual map creation is a known weak point; use narrative descriptions, simple diagrams, and player handouts based on text for now. Starter area (Ravensport districts + docks + one nearby dead zone) is fully detailed for self-contained play.
- NPCs: Key quest-relevant NPCs pre-created below with full details per guideline. Master NPC file updated.
- Branching: The medallion recovery is the inciting incident. Player choices (keep it, turn it in, ignore side hooks, pursue rumors) determine path. DM can weave paths together (e.g., smuggling ring involved in medallion theft; mining delivery uncovers related sabotage).
- Beacon Core Note (Sweeping Update): Dead zones are caused by large beacon cores (Residuum + Mithral + specialized rune-engraved crystals) that absorb and syphon magic to Aetheron. Secret high-magic zone (~20 ft around core): advantage on spells, +2d10 heal/damage, effects last 10× longer. This is high-level/secret.

## Session 0: Character Creation + The Rusty Anchor Hook

**Goals**:
- Character introductions, backstories tied to Ravensport/gates/dead zones/trade.
- Establish core world elements: Dimensional gates, magic dead zones (one ~2 hours walk west of city), racial/cultural dynamics, Aetherite technology as rare/expensive, beacon absorption mechanics.
- Deliver first job: Recover the stolen Resonance Medallion for Captain Rurik.
- Plant seeds for all branching paths via rumors, staff interactions, and the item itself.
- End with clear next steps and player agency.

**Structure Outline**:
1. **Arrival at The Rusty Anchor (Docks District)**: Lively tavern. Describe smells, sounds, crowd.
2. **Meet Captain Elandor “Blackscale” Rurik**: Grizzled human with eyepatch. He gives the pitch.
3. **Initial Roleplay & Rumors**: Players can talk to staff or patrons. Lirael "Silverthorn" drops hints about smuggling, western troubles, or odd mechanical issues.
4. **The Theft Details**: Box stolen from strongroom. Client is "very important" (linked to Veyne’s network).
5. **Reward & Hook**: 50 gp upfront, 150 gp on return + room/board + favors. Medallion description given (ordinary silver necklace with hidden blue crystal).
6. **Investigation Start**: Search docks, interview staff, or head to Bazaar / Shrouded Wards.
7. **End of Session 0**: Decision point with the medallion.

**Key Decision Point**: Keep/study it (dangerous path) or turn it in.

## Branching Quest Paths (Sessions 1–5)

**Path 1: The Resonance Medallion – Keep or Study It**  
**Development**: Identification at Arcane Emporium or testing near dead zone reveals properties. Leads to Aetherbound pursuit and beacon core hints (high-magic zone discovery).

**Path 2: Turn In the Medallion for Reward**  
**Development**: Client complications or new jobs. Links to smuggling or mining paths.

**Path 3: Delivery Job to Deepanvil (Western Path)**  
**Development**: Caravan delivery of non-magical dwarven engineering equipment. Mine subquests (cave-ins, sabotage, mechanical anomalies near beacon core). Cargo: Reinforced mechanical drills, precision tools, vibration-dampening vessels (no anti-magic tools unless sabotage plot).

**Path 4: Investigate the Smuggling Ring in Shrouded Wards**  
**Development**: Urban intrigue. Ring moving Aetherite-related items or gate contraband. Ties to medallion or beacon tech.

**Convergence**: All paths lead to beacon core discoveries and Veyne’s plans.
